Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

At Plainly, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the Plainly app.

1. Data Collection

Plainly is designed to collect as little data as possible. We do not require you to create an account to use the application. Any content you share with Plainly (text, links, images, or documents) is used solely for the purpose of providing you with an explanation or summary.

2. Data Processing

To provide high-quality explanations, Plainly uses a combination of on-device processing and secure cloud-based AI services (such as Google Gemini). When cloud processing is required:

  • Your data is transmitted securely via encrypted connections.
  • Your data is used only to generate the requested summary or explanation.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models or for any purpose other than providing the service to you.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers. The only third-party services used are those necessary for the core functionality of the app (e.g., AI processing providers), and they are bound by strict confidentiality and security requirements.

4. Data Storage

Your history is stored locally on your device. You can clear your history at any time through the app settings. We do not maintain a permanent record of your processed content on our servers beyond what is necessary for immediate processing.

5. Your Choices

You have full control over your data. Since your history is stored on-device, you can delete it at any time. If you choose not to share content with the app, the core functionality will not be available, but your privacy remains protected.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@plainly.app.
